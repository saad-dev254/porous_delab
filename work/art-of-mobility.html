Art of Mobility

SP/RES_16
Year
2023
Location
Guggenheim Museum, Bilbao Spain
Collaborations
University of Cape Town, Norman Foster Foundation
About

The Art of Mobility is a speculative design that re-imagines safe and flexible mobility in African cities. The contemporary vision was featured at Norman Foster Foundation's Motion exhibition at the Guggenheim Museum in Spain.
